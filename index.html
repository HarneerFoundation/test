Who We Are

In Good Books of Nature

Harneer Foundation is a registered grassroots non-profit working at the intersection of environmental restoration, community empowerment, and social equity. With core values of selfless service and country-first thinking, we deliver visible, quantified impact where it matters most — at the ground level.

Our Foundation

Purpose, Mission & Vision

Our Aim
What We Strive For

To undertake environmental conservation and restoration, plantations, rainwater harvesting, and groundwater recharging to deliver visible, quantified impact on ecosystems, wildlife habitat, biodiversity, and climate change. To assist in education, health, sanitation, and water needs of marginal communities, tribals, BPL families, and the needy.

Our Mission
How We Work

To work toward a sustainable future where the environment is protected, restored, and conserved for the benefit of all — while improving the quality of life of marginal and neglected communities, especially in rural areas. We bridge the gap between ecological resilience and human dignity.

Our Vision
Where We're Going

To become the nation's top non-profit leader, generating visible and quantified impact on climate change, ecosystems, wildlife habitat, biodiversity, and the social canvas of India — inspiring a movement of grassroot environmental stewardship across every district.

Core Programs

Three Pillars of Impact

Every initiative we undertake falls under one of our three transformative pillars — connecting ecological health with human flourishing.

🌍
Climate Action & Biodiversity

Promoting eco-friendly practices and reforestation to combat climate change. Establishing nurseries of indigenous trees, creating green cover and forests, and developing wildlife habitats to restore the natural balance.

Reforestation Wildlife Habitat Indigenous Nurseries Carbon Sequestration
💧
Water Conservation

Creating and restoring rainwater harvesters and groundwater recharge systems. Construction of ponds, tanks, and pipelines using modern practices to ensure water access for communities and ecosystems alike.

Rainwater Harvesting Groundwater Recharge Ponds & Tanks Water Security
🤝
Social Action & Empowerment

Addressing health, education, and livelihood for tribals and BPL communities. Promoting sustainable agriculture, waste management, renewable energy, and participating in government schemes for rural welfare.

Women Empowerment Skill Development Rural Health Sustainable Farming
